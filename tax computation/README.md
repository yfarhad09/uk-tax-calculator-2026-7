# UK Self-employment tax calculator
A command line tool for calculating the total tax liability based in 2026/27 tax year rates

## What it does

This program requests user input for the income, expenses, and capital allowances for the tax year and outputs the National Insurance, Income Tax, and total Tax Liability (the sum of NI and Income Tax)

## Requirements

Python 3

## How to run it

```
python tax_computation.py
```

## Inputs

- **Income** - your total gross income for the year

- **Expenses** - your total allowable expenses for the year

- **Capital Allowances** - your capital allowances for the year

## Limitations

This program is based on the rates and bands for the 2026/27 tax year.
